About Me

Welcome! I'm Zachary Nicholas, a multidisciplinary technologist with expertise in mechanical troubleshooting, cybersecurity operations, and software development. My career has been shaped by structured problem-solving, risk mitigation, and a drive for innovation across multiple technical disciplines.

Military Experience

During my time in the United States Marine Corps, I specialized in heavy equipment maintenance and diagnostics, working across electrical systems, refrigeration, automotive maintenance, and water support. My responsibilities included:

  • Technical publication management and compliance enforcement
  • Inventory tracking via GCSS-MC for logistics control and parts requisitioning
  • Structured troubleshooting and operational readiness reporting to leadership
  • Hands-on diagnostics of hydraulic, pneumatic, and mechanical systems

College Projects

Building on my military experience, I expanded into software development and cybersecurity during my time in college. Some key projects include:

  • Designed and hosted a full-stack web application in AWS, demonstrating cloud-based infrastructure deployment.
  • Developed automated security testing workflows, leveraging Python and Selenium for penetration testing.
  • Implemented secure coding practices aligned with NIST security standards.
  • Configured and optimized Linux & Windows operating systems for performance and security.
  • Built embedded system applications using Texas Instruments microcontrollers for real-time calibration.

Current Focus

Today, I am focused on security management, systems administration, and interdisciplinary problem-solving, while also exploring engineering disciplines such as aerospace, cyber-physical systems, and automation technology. My diverse background—bridging mechanical expertise, cybersecurity principles, and structured software development—allows me to approach technical challenges with precision, adaptability, and innovation.

If you're passionate about technology, security, or engineering, let's connect—I’d love to collaborate and exchange ideas!
